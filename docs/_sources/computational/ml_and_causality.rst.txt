Machine learning and causal inference
=========================================

Recommended courses at UiO:

- `IN5400/IN9400 Machine Learning for Image Analysis <https://www.uio.no/studier/emner/matnat/ifi/IN5400/index-eng.html>`_
- `STK-IN9300 Statistical Learning Methods in Data Science <https://www.uio.no/studier/emner/matnat/math/STK-IN9300/>`_

Recommended seminars at UiO:

- `Research Seminar at the Section for Machine Learning, Department of Informatics <https://www.mn.uio.no/ifi/english/about/organisation/mls/seminar/>`_

Recommended books:

- `Deep Learning book <https://www.deeplearningbook.org/>`_ by Goodfellow et al. 2016
- `The Elements of Statistical Learning <https://web.stanford.edu/~hastie/ElemStatLearn/>`_ by Hastie et al. 2008

Relevant papers:

- `Model Evaluation, Model Selection, and Algorithm Selection in Machine Learning <http://arxiv.org/abs/1811.12808>`_ by Raschka 2018

- `DOME: recommendations for supervised machine learning validattion in biology <https://www.nature.com/articles/s41592-021-01205-4>`_ by Walsh et al. 2021

- `An introduction to domain adaptation and transfer learning <http://arxiv.org/abs/1812.11806>`_ by Kouw and Loog 2018

- `Causality for Machine Learning <http://arxiv.org/abs/1911.10500>`_ by Schölkopf 2019

- `Causal Inference and the Data-Fusion Problem <https://www.pnas.org/content/113/27/7345>`_ by Bareinboim and Pearl 2016

- `Towards Causal Representation Learning <https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/9363924>`_ by Schölkopf et al. 2021
